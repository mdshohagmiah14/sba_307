About Us

At Per Scholas, we believe a thriving workforce starts with equitable access to education. Learn about Per Scholas and how we provide skills training and access to employer networks to individuals often excluded from tech careers. Per Scholas envisions a technology workforce as diverse as the customers it serves. More than 25,000 graduates have launched successful careers in tech to date through our no-cost technical training. Our mission is to advance economic equity through rigorous training for tech careers and to connect skilled talent to leading businesses.

Feature Details
Tuition-free training All courses are offered at no cost to learners.
Curriculum focus: Training covers areas like IT support, cybersecurity, cloud computing, often leading to certifications like CompTIA A+.
Career development support Includes professional development workshops, resume writing, mock interviews, and dedicated career coaching
Employer network Connects graduates with a network of companies looking to hire diverse tech talent.
Targeted demographics We focus on supporting women, people of color, veterans, immigrants, and individuals facing long-term unemployment.
